Who is ghost?

Ghost is a swedish rock band that formed in 2006. Unlike otherbands they have alot of lore. In the lore universe, ghost is an devil-worshipping theistic satanic church that is conducting rituals over the world in order to spread their massage. They pose as a band to become populare and reach more poeple. The Cleregy controls the ministry with Sister Imperator as their qurent leader. The lead singer of the band is a satanic pope like figure, who gain the title Papa Emeritus. There has been 4 papa Emeritus, Cardinal Copia or Papa Emeritus IV is the curent one. The band also consist of many nameless ghouls. The fanbase usually give them headcanon names. the ghouls get switched out from time to time, some of the passed once had canon names.

In 2010 Ghost relesed the single "Elizabeth" that became their first hit, Shortly after they released the album "Opus Emponymous" feeturing the "Elizabeth" song.

Infestissuman

Opus Emponymous ended with the track "Genesis", about the birth of Antichrist, and Infestissuman continiuse the antichrists birth and onwards. The album was released on April 10. 2013, and won the Grammis Award for Best Hard Rock/Metal in 2014. Later they relesed a Redux of the album.

Meliora

Ghost third album "Meliora" was released 21th may 2015. It was revealed that Papa Emeritus II was fired, in an advertisement of the album. His succesor, Papa Emeritus III was his younger brother by 3 months. It has later been made a Redux of Meliora. This is one of my favorite albums.

Prequelle

Prequel was released on 1 June 2018, and was nominated for The Best Rock Album Grammy Award at the 61st Annual Awards Ceremony and the Swedish Grammy Award in the category of hardrock/metal. This was the fist album to be recorded after the lead singer and actor of the different papas was officialy confirmed to be Tobias Forge.
